Middle-class families face overwhelming legal complexity navigating document reviews, form preparation, tenant/employment rights disputes, and family law issues. Intimidation and lack of guidance lead to forfeited justice and financial losses.

The Solution

CommonLaw AI deploys four specialized AI agents working in concert:

  • Document Explainer: Plain-English contract/lease analysis
  • Form Assistant: K-12 pathway optimization
  • Rights Educator: Personalized legal rights guidance
  • Case Strategist: Small claims/dispute preparation

Technology Stack: GPT-4/LangChain AI agents with legal corpus training, integrated document parsing, court filing APIs. GDPR compliant, attorney-client privilege protections.
